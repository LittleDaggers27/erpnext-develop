# Security Policy

The Quantum ERP team and community take security issues seriously. To report a security issue, fill out the form at [https://erp-quantum.com/security/report](https://erp-quantum.com/security/report).

You can help us make Quantum ERP and all it's users more secure by following the [Reporting guidelines](https://erp-quantum.com/security).

We appreciate your efforts to responsibly disclose your findings. We'll endeavor to respond quickly, and will keep you updated throughout the process.
